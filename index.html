About Me!

My Background

Now I start my journey into this world, and I'll begin by presenting you this landing page!

Work And Education

My Work

Hospitality

The majority of my working lifetime as a young adault has been spent mostly working in the service and hospitality industry, fulfilling a variety of different roles. During this period I worked in a variety of different bars, restaurants, cafes and take-aways. Most notable of them were definitly The Ivy Dawson Street, Glas Restaurant and Dominos Pizza. Working as floor staff in restaurants always came very naturally to me and I felt that I brought a lot to offer my team, as I was often asked to serve customers of famous or high profile status. Over time several establishments I had worked for offered me promotions within a few months of being there, some of which I gladly accepted, and others refused due to not being to commit at the time. After Finishing college I decided to take a break from working in hospitailty and test my arm in a different career path. Hospitality taught me a huge amount of skills (time management, team work, communication, customer service/satisfaction, leadership), about work and business, and even a bit about myself


Retail

Growing up as a teenager, I also spent quite a bit of time working in retail on weekends and in the summers, primarily within the supermarket and food retail sector. Retail and service were similar enough in some ways, such as customer satisfaction and quality control, however the business models and scopes were vastly different. Logistics management, stock control and income accounts were all handled very differently, but gave the interest to want to learn more of the inner working of a business and how its all kept running. The skills I learned there still help me to this day, and I hope to use them in better practice for a business of my own.


Sales

After completing college I decided to make a bit of a career change from the hospitality industry and move into sales for time. I felt that my customer service and upselling skills I had learned from hospitality would translate rather easily into a sales position. I started shortly after as a junior sales executive with Frank Keane Volkswagen here in Dublin. The job was exciting as we started with some sales training in the form of Symco. road to sales and sales fitness courses. However soon after starting with FKVW the pandemic took place and the motor industry was brought to a halt. I continued my position as junior sales for several months after, but with what few sales coming through going to the senior sales team, I decided to put my skills elsewhere for the time. That said I am keen to take another stab at some sales opportunities.
